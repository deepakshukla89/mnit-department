Brief Research Profile

He has published more than 80 research papers in journals and conference proceedings. Some of the publications are in IJPE, EAAI, IJPR, PPC, IJAMT, Computers and Industrial Engineering etc. He has successfully Completed a DST Project on Development of a multi-agent based system for dynamic multi-project scheduling as Principal Investigator. He is reviewer of international journals such as IJPR.
